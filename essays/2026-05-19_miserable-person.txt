i know a woman who always complains about life, her kids, her husband, and the absence of a job.

i asked, “why don’t you divorce him, leave the kids, and find a job?”
she said, “are you crazy? i can’t.”

i asked, “why? if you hate them so much and want to make money, why can’t you?”
she said, “you don’t understand...”

i believe this is what misery looks like: a state where you know exactly what the problem is and understand exactly what is happening, yet you close your eyes and let entropy accumulate further.

i do not want to be heartless. i perfectly understand that sometimes people are expected to simply listen, but i deeply believe that human destiny is in our own hands, and that we must be ruthless enough to control it with an iron fist. feedback is meant to be learned from and acted upon, not turned into endless complaints.

simply: do not cling to what destroys you. move fast, cut things off, and take control of your own life. make decisions regardless of consequences or fear. bravery is not the absence of fear, but the ability to act despite it.

more simply: your life depends on you.
